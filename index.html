Bongo-Cat Creator

Create your own bongo cat by uploading a track and using keyboard input to make bongocat play. Screen + audio record to put together your final video.

Open on desktop to get started.

Bongo-Cat Creator

Instructions:

Upload a song below and press keys on your keyboard to have #bongocat play along. You can use a tool like Quicktime to do a screen and audio recording!

Default song is from Mario Paint (:

  (mp3, m4a, or wav)
Keypressed:
Possible keyboard input: a, s, d, f, j, k, space, enter
